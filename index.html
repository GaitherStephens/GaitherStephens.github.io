Profession

Currently, I am a Data Analyst/Systems Administrator at Gulf Coast Partnership, a not for profit organization that serves as lead agency for other non-profits serving those in need throughout Florida. I work with the CIS (Community Information System) checking data quality, ensuring data completeness, and administrating the ServicePoint system. I also provide IT support for The Homeless Coalition in Charlotte County.

About Me

I'm originally from Muncie, Indiana, spent about ten years in Cincinnati, Ohio, and now live in Port Charlotte, Florida. I managed radio stations for the better part of two decades and recently began pursuing a career in database administration. I earned my AS in Computer Science & Information Systems from Purdue Univeristy in 2006, my BS Business Administration from Indiana Wesleyean in 2013, and my MS in Computer Information Systems from Boston University in 2016.
